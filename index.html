About Us

Gamji Global Concept is a forward-thinking company delivering reliable services in telecommunications, digital identity, and financial exchange. Our strategic partnership with MTN Nigeria supports KYC verification, SIM activations, and NIN enrollment. Our Bureau de Change offers trustworthy and competitive foreign exchange services. We combine regulatory compliance, ethical standards, and innovation to serve customers across Nigeria and beyond.

Our Services

  • General SIM Solutions – SIM activations, swaps, upgrades, and troubleshooting.
  • NIN Enrollment – Official biometric and data capture for national identity.
  • KYC Onboarding – Verified identity checks in collaboration with MTN and Sidrachain.
  • Bureau de Change – Trusted currency exchange for USD, EUR, GBP, CFA, etc.
  • Closed User Group (CUG) – Discounted internal calling and data sharing for teams and businesses.
  • Laptop and Accessories Supply – Sales of branded laptops and essential computing tools.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in telecom, digital identity, and currency exchange solutions—connecting people and businesses through secure and innovative services.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver fast, secure, and reliable services that support connectivity, identity, and global commerce through partnerships, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Global Presence

While based in Nigeria, we maintain a global outlook, extending services to diaspora communities, travelers, and international partners through digital platforms and partnerships.
