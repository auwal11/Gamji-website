About Us

Gamji Global Concept is a leading service provider offering digital solutions across Nigeria. Located in Jalingo, Taraba State, our mission is to make technology accessible through services like SIM registration, NIN enrollment, and more.

Our Services

  • General SIM Solutions: Full support for SIM registration, swaps, and updates.
  • Bureau de Change: Secure and reliable currency exchange service.
  • Online Registration: Assisting clients with form filling and official online processes.
  • NIN Enrollment: Fast and verified national identity number registration.

  • Laptop and Supply: Providing quality laptops and tech accessories.
