It involves collecting biometric and demographic data such as fingerprints, photographs, date of birth, and residential address. This number is essential for accessing various services like SIM card registration, banking, public service benefits, and more. Proper enrollment ensures an individual’s information is securely stored and linked across governmental and commercial platforms, strengthening national identity and reducing fraud.
